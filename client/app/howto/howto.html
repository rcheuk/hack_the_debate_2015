How to Play Candidate Clicker

  1. Click on the candidate for whom the statement at the top most applies. As much as you’d like!
  2. See how your clicks compare with all users and try to click your candidate to the top.
  3. Watching the debate live?
    • Clicks counted while the debate is airing will count more, with the potential to earn click multipliers based on what’s happening during the debate.
    • Be on the lookout for _____, when clicks will subtract from the total number that candidate has rather than add to them.
    • Earn even more click multipliers when you share Candidate Clicker.
  4. Click click click!
